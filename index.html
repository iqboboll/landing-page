The highest peak in Southeast Asia, offering breathtaking views and challenging hikes.

Tunku Abdul Rahman Park

A marine park consisting of five islands, perfect for snorkeling and diving.

Sipadan Island

Renowned for its rich marine biodiversity, making it a top diving destination.

Danum Valley

A pristine rainforest reserve, home to diverse wildlife and ancient trees.
